CSCI426 - Game Prototyping

Course Description: Students will design and implement 8 videogame prototypes in the first 10 weeks in weekly rotating teams of two (prototype phase). We will announce the teams and design prompt for the following week at the end of each class. Thereafter we will form teams of approx. 4 students around the best prototypes, followed by a final polish cycle of 4 weeks, in which these teams will iterate on one of the prototypes in order to polish and balance the game (production phase).
